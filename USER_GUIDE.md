# User Guide

Welcome to DarshanEase! This guide will help you navigate the platform as a devotee.

## 1. Finding a Temple
- Navigate to the **Temples** page from the top navigation bar.
- Use the **Search Bar** to look for specific temples by name, city, or state.
- Click **View Details** on a temple card to read its history, see facilities, and check timings.

## 2. Booking a Darshan Slot
1. On the Temple Details page, scroll down to the **Book Darshan** section.
2. Select a Date and Time slot from the list. Wait for the continue button to activate.
3. Select the number of devotees and fill in the details for each person (Name, Age, ID Proof).
4. Review your booking. If the slot requires a ticket price, the mock payment gateway will appear.
5. Click **Confirm & Pay**.
6. Once successful, you will receive a Booking Confirmation with a downloadable QR-code enabled PDF Ticket.

## 3. Managing Your Bookings
- Click on **Dashboard** in the top navigation.
- Your upcoming and past bookings will be listed.
- You can **Cancel** a booking here if needed. (Note: Canceling will instantly refund and restore the seat capacity).
- Click **Download Ticket** to re-download your PDF ticket.

## 4. Leaving a Review
- After completing a Darshan (or having a confirmed booking), you can leave a review.
- Go to your Dashboard, navigate to the specific booking, and write a review and give a star rating.
